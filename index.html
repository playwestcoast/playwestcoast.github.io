About us

This event grew out of some ideas bounced around by the KublaCon-attending contingent of “Legendary EastBay Gamers” (aka LEG) an informal game group that meets mainly in Oakland, California. We wanted to create a unique gaming opportunity for a wider cross-section of gamers in Northern California and beyond.

 

[kublacon group shot]

 

We hope you'll join us for our first PlayFest event. We're already looking forward to 2018 and beyond and experiences and feedback this November will really help shape the future of this endeavour.
