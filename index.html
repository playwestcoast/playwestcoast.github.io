PlayWest Events

Unique Bay Area and destination tabletop gaming events.
Both by-invitation and public.

What we do

We put together 1-to-2 day gaming events in the Bay Area, as well as destination events like our Sierra mountian retreat. Events focus on open play of modern board and card games, as well as creating a welcoming environment as stated in our code of conduct

Schedule

  • December 29 – Holiday GameFest – Oakland, Public
  • April 27 – Tabletop Day – Oakland, Public
  • June/July TBD – Summer GameFest – Oakland, Invite-only
  • October 11-13 – Sierra PlayFest – Tahoe City, Invite-first
